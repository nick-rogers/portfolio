About Me

Hi, I’m Nick. I’m a senior at Washington University in
St. Louis, about to cap off my education in Communication
design. I’ve got many areas of interest, within and outside
of the design discipline including UI/UX, branding, human-
centered-design and history (I’m a history double-major). I
relish in opportunities to combine my areas of interest
and pick up new passions along the way.

Whether I’m researching a confounding period of human
history or thinking through the correct brand positioning
for a corporation or (more often) debugging a website or
program, complexity and making sense of complexity is
what drives me. I see opportunity in dense and entangled
networks of information and behavior, and I would love to
apply this outlook to designing clear, effective, and
enjoyable human-technology relationships.
